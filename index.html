About

I am a PhD student and graduate research assistant at the University of North Dakota, working under the supervision of Dr. Ahmed Abdelhadi. My research bridges quantum computing and wireless communications, with a focus on making quantum algorithms practically viable on near-term hardware and applying them to real network problems.

Before my current focus on quantum systems, I contributed to foundational work on RSMA, RIS, and ISAC for 6G networks, published in IEEE Access. I am currently preparing two conference papers for IEEE CARS 2026, hosted at UND.
